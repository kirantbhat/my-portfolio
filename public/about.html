About Me

Whether it's troubleshooting complex outages or planning a seamless network migration, I approach each challenge with precision, urgency, and a commitment to minimizing downtime. I thrive in collaborative environments but can also work independently under pressure.

Above all, I’m passionate about building systems that empower people and businesses to stay connected, efficient, and secure.
